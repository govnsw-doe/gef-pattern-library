  • Show/hides enable users to quickly scan titles and select one to reveal its content.
  • Multiple items can be opened at any one time.
  • Show/hides are a good way to create distinct information blocks on a content page.
  • Titles should be short to medium in length (up to 70 characters).
  • Titles should be descriptive of the content it contains so users can easily scan and decide.
  • Keep content bite-sized and quick to read.
  • Keep the amount of content similar across show/hides to create a consistent rhythm.
  • Avoid including many links within a content block as they will be difficult to find.
  • Long show/hides should be split into two or more groups. Give each group a relevant heading.
  • Avoid using show/hides for FAQ-style content.

Accessibility

  • Use TAB key to enter a group of show/hide items.
  • TAB to move between show/hide titles.
  • ENTER to open or close a show/hide item.
  • After an item is opened, TAB to focus on its content block.
